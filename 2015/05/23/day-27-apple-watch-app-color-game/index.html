Day 27 – Apple Watch App – Color Game

23 May 2015


Description: a color game made for Apple Watch. Source Code

Features:

  • Easy to play color game, just choose the right matching colors and you’ll get a score increase
  • Use force touch to trigger the menu to stop game, check scores or set the playing time

Notes:

  • It’s a known problem that the watch app runs quite slowly. I tried to incorporate framework, but it just slows down the app a lot. And sometimes, it stops at the loading screen.
  • I also tried to pay a “Show Score” button at the first screen, ironically half the time the app will stop at the loading screen. So I moved it to settings
  • I realized that I had some spelling problem with the app, too much waiting for the loading screen to change, so I just let it go.
